Dr. Brian J. Gallagher
Physical Therapist PT, DPT, MS

Dr. Brian J. Gallagher is a proud father, husband, and son and a native of Northeast Ohio. He earned a bachelor's degree in exercise physiology and a master's degree in sport science from Ohio University, followed by his Doctorate in Physical Therapy from American International College. Brian is post-graduate trained in Dry Needling and Spinal Manipulation. He was a member of the 2004 Ohio University Men's Ice Hockey National Championship team and was inducted into the Ohio University Athletic Hall of Fame in 2010. Brian played two seasons of professional ice hockey in Finland from 2004 to 2006 and enjoys fishing, hunting, skiing, and surfing.

Garrett Gellerman
Physical Therapist DPT, ACSM-EP

Garrett is originally from Valley Springs, California, where his love for sports sparked his passion for physical therapy. He specialises in orthopaedics and is certified as an Exercise Physiologist through the American College of Sports Medicine. With six years of experience, Garrett is dedicated to helping patients regain strength, mobility, and confidence through personalised, evidence-based care. Outside of work, he enjoys golfing and spending time with his wife Kimberly and son Elliott.
