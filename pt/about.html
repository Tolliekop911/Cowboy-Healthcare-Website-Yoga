Our Story

Recovery built on
real expertise

Cowboy Healthcare was founded in 2014 in Corpus Christi, Texas, by Dr. Brian J. Gallagher, DPT, and has grown into a trusted physical therapy and therapeutic yoga practice serving patients across Texas and Ohio.

Cowboy Healthcare was founded in 2014 in Corpus Christi, Texas, by Dr. Brian J. Gallagher, DPT. We began by providing physical therapy services to South Texas Surgical Hospital, a physician-owned 32-bed inpatient acute care specialty hospital. From those early days serving patients through acute surgical recovery, we grew a complementary outpatient practice focused on delivering seamless continuity of care as patients transitioned from hospital to home and community life.

For more than 12 years, Cowboy Healthcare has been a trusted provider across the Texas Coastal Bend, building strong relationships with patients and referring physicians. Today, we continue that commitment by delivering specialized post-operative physical therapy services at Legent Outpatient Surgery, Corpus Christi's premier boutique outpatient surgical center, in close coordination with the skilled orthopedic surgeons at The Orthopedic Center of Corpus Christi.

In 2025, we expanded northward and opened our outpatient physical therapy and therapeutic yoga practice in Ravenna, Ohio. Here we blend evidence-based clinical expertise with the restorative benefits of therapeutic yoga, offering patients a holistic approach to recovery, mobility, pain management, and long-term wellness in a welcoming, community-centered setting.

Who We Are

Care that comes
to you

Cowboy Healthcare was built on a straightforward idea: patients recovering from surgery, managing chronic pain, or preparing for a procedure should not have to fight traffic or navigate clinics when they are at their most vulnerable.

Our licensed physical therapy specialists travel to patients across Ohio, delivering the same clinical-grade treatment available in the best rehabilitation centres, in the comfort and safety of your home.

We work closely with referring physicians, surgeons, and care coordinators to ensure that every treatment plan is aligned with your broader medical picture, your environment, and your goals.

Our Journey

How we got here

2014

Founded in Texas

Cowboy Healthcare launched in Corpus Christi, Texas, providing physical therapy services at South Texas Surgical Hospital, a physician-owned 32-bed inpatient acute care specialty hospital.

→

Outpatient Growth & Continuity

We expanded into outpatient services to ensure patients received consistent, high-quality care from the acute hospital setting through full recovery and return to daily life.
