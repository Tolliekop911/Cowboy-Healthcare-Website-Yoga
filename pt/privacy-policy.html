Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

Cowboy Healthcare, LLC ("Cowboy Healthcare," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Cowboy Healthcare Physical Therapy and Cowboy Yoga websites and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our websites or use our services.

By using our websites or services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you contact us or book an appointment.
  • Appointment and booking data — dates, times, service preferences, and scheduling details submitted through our booking system.
  • Health information — only when voluntarily provided in the context of scheduling physical therapy services, handled under applicable healthcare privacy laws.
  • Usage data — browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referring URL, collected automatically via standard web server logs.

How We Use Your Information

  • Schedule, confirm, and manage appointments
  • Respond to inquiries and provide patient support
  • Send appointment reminders and service-related communications
  • Improve the performance and content of our websites
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Health Information (HIPAA)

As a physical therapy provider, Cowboy Healthcare is subject to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Health information you share in the context of receiving physical therapy services is protected under our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, provided at your first appointment and available upon request. Our EHR system processes data according to its own security and privacy standards.

Cookies

Our websites use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some site features may not function correctly. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information by contacting us at info@cowboyhealthcare.com. Health record requests are processed under HIPAA guidelines.
