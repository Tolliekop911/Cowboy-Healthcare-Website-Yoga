Careers at
Cowboy Healthcare

We are a growing physical therapy practice built on clinical excellence, genuine patient relationships, and the belief that great care is personal. If that sounds like you, we want to hear from you.

01

One-on-One Care

We see patients individually — no double-booking, no aides. Every session is 100% therapist time with the patient.

02

Autonomous Practice

Our therapists have full clinical autonomy to design treatment plans without administrative interference or productivity quotas.

03

Competitive Compensation

Compensation that reflects your credentials and experience, with performance-based growth as the practice expands.

04

Continuing Education

We invest in post-graduate training — dry needling, spinal manipulation, and emerging techniques.
