What We Do

Precision care for
bones, joints & muscle

Orthopedic physical therapy focuses on the musculoskeletal system — the bones, joints, muscles, tendons, and ligaments that keep you moving. When any part of that system breaks down, the ripple effect touches everything: your sleep, your work, your independence.

At Cowboy Healthcare, our licensed Doctors of Physical Therapy specialise in restoring that system. Whether you're recovering from a hip replacement, managing knee arthritis, or healing a rotator cuff tear, we design treatment plans built around your specific anatomy, your surgical history, and your goals.

We don't do cookie-cutter programmes. Every plan is built from scratch after a thorough evaluation — because what works for one patient's knee may be exactly wrong for another's.

Conditions We Treat

If it hurts to move,
we can help

🦵

Knee & Hip Replacement Recovery

Structured, progressive rehabilitation following total or partial knee and hip replacements. We coordinate with your surgical team to follow approved protocols and accelerate your return to function.

💪

Rotator Cuff & Shoulder Injuries

Tendon tears, impingement, and post-surgical shoulder rehab. We restore shoulder mobility, strength, and stability — whether you've had surgery or are managing conservatively.

🦴

Fracture Rehabilitation

Once cleared by your physician, we rebuild strength and range of motion around healed fractures — wrist, ankle, clavicle, pelvis, and more — reducing the risk of secondary complications.

🔗

ACL & Ligament Reconstruction

Evidence-based return-to-function protocols following ACL, MCL, and PCL reconstruction. We track strength symmetry milestones to guide your progression safely.

🌀

Osteoarthritis & Joint Pain

Therapeutic exercise and manual therapy to reduce arthritis pain, improve joint mobility, and slow functional decline — without relying solely on medication or injections.

📐

Spinal & Back Conditions

Disc herniations, spinal stenosis, spondylolisthesis, and post-spinal surgery rehab. We target the source of pain with manual therapy, core stabilisation, and movement re-education.

Our Approach

What to expect
at your first visit

Every orthopedic patient starts with a comprehensive evaluation. Here's how it works:

01
Full Evaluation (60 min)We review your surgical or injury history, assess range of motion, strength, posture, and functional movement patterns. We listen first — your goals shape the plan.
02
Custom Treatment PlanBased on your evaluation, your DPT designs a personalised programme — typically 2–3 sessions per week. Every plan includes hands-on therapy, targeted exercise, and a home programme.
03
Hands-On TreatmentManual therapy, joint mobilisation, dry needling, therapeutic exercise, and modalities as appropriate. We use the tools that evidence supports — not the ones we've always done.
04
Progress TrackingWe reassess regularly, communicate with your referring physician, and adjust your plan as you improve — not on a fixed schedule, but based on your actual progress.
05
Discharge & IndependenceYou leave with the tools to maintain your gains. We don't aim for dependency — we aim to make ourselves unnecessary.
