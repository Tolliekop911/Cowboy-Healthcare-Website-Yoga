Why Post-Op PT Matters

Surgery is the start,
not the finish

Studies consistently show that patients who complete structured post-operative physical therapy recover faster, regain more function, and experience fewer complications than those who rely on rest alone. The quality of your PT matters as much as the quality of your surgery.

At Cowboy Healthcare, we work directly with your surgical team to understand your procedure, your surgeon's specific protocol, and your precautions. Every programme is built around those specifics — not a generic template.

We begin as early as your surgeon allows — often within days of your procedure — because early, appropriate movement is one of the most powerful tools we have to reduce swelling, prevent stiffness, and set the trajectory for full recovery.

Surgeries We Rehabilitate

We coordinate with
your surgical team

🦵

Total Knee Replacement

Progressive range-of-motion restoration, quadriceps strengthening, and gait retraining. We follow approved protocols and aim to get you walking independently and climbing stairs within weeks.

🦴

Total Hip Replacement

Hip precaution education, early mobility, and progressive strengthening. We reduce fall risk, restore normal gait pattern, and return you to independent daily activities.

🔧

Spinal Surgery

Post-laminectomy, discectomy, spinal fusion, and decompression rehab. We protect the surgical site while rebuilding core stability, flexibility, and functional movement.

💪

Shoulder & Rotator Cuff Surgery

Phase-based shoulder rehab following rotator cuff repair, labral repair, or total shoulder replacement. Passive to active to resistive progression matched to tissue healing timelines.

🩺

Abdominal & Bariatric Surgery

Early mobility, breathing exercises, and progressive functional restoration following abdominal procedures including bariatric surgery, hernia repair, and colostomy procedures.

🦶

Foot, Ankle & Lower Leg Surgery

Post-surgical rehab for Achilles tendon repair, ankle reconstruction, bunionectomy, and foot fusion. Weight-bearing progressions per surgeon clearance.

Our Protocol

Phase by phase.
Week by week.

01
Pre-Discharge CoordinationWhenever possible, we connect with your surgical team before discharge so your PT programme begins immediately — no gap in care, no lost time.
02
Acute Phase (Weeks 1–2)Swelling management, pain reduction, safe range-of-motion initiation, and early strengthening within surgical restrictions. We build a home exercise routine you can start the same day.
03
Rebuilding Phase (Weeks 3–8)Progressive loading, functional movement retraining, and gait or upper-extremity normalisation depending on your surgery. We advance based on your tissue response, not a fixed schedule.
04
Strengthening Phase (Weeks 8–16+)Return to full activities of daily life, stairs, driving, work tasks, and recreational activities. Higher-demand patients continue to sport-specific or occupation-specific training.
05
Discharge PlanningYou leave with a maintenance programme and clear signs to watch for. We communicate a final progress report to your surgeon and are available if questions arise down the road.
