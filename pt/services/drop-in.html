Why Cash Pay

The benefits of
bypassing insurance

No Authorisation Delays

Insurance pre-authorisation can take days or weeks. Cash-pay patients are seen the same day or next day — when the injury is fresh and the window for fastest recovery is open.

More Time with Your Therapist

Insurance-covered PT is subject to productivity requirements that shorten your hands-on time. Cash sessions are fully therapist-led — no aide handoffs, no shortened visits.

You Choose Your Focus

No ICD-10 codes, no approved diagnoses. You can come in for performance optimisation, injury prevention, post-marathon recovery, or anything else — no documentation required.

Good to Know

Cash-pay sessions are paid at time of service. We accept credit/debit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and cash. Cash-pay PT is generally HSA/FSA-eligible — check with your plan administrator. We can provide a detailed receipt (superbill) for patients who wish to self-submit to their insurance for out-of-network reimbursement. Prices above are standard rates and may be adjusted; call us to confirm current pricing before booking.
