Our Story

Yoga rooted in healthcare

Cowboy Yoga is the therapeutic movement program of Cowboy Healthcare, bringing evidence-based yoga to patients and community members across Ohio.

Where clinical care meets movement

Cowboy Healthcare was built on the belief that recovery does not stop when you leave the clinic. Therapeutic yoga extend that care into daily life, helping patients rebuild strength, restore mobility, and manage pain through mindful, evidence-based movement.

Our yoga program is not a standalone fitness offering. It is an integrated part of Cowboy Healthcare's continuum of care, designed and supervised by licensed physical therapy specialists who understand the body at a clinical level.

Whether you are recovering from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or simply looking to move better and feel stronger, Cowboy Yoga meets you exactly where you are.

What sets us apart

01

Clinical Foundation

Every class is informed by physical therapy principles. Our instructors understand anatomy, injury, and rehabilitation at a professional level.

02

Every Body Welcome

From post-surgical patients to seasoned practitioners, our classes are designed to be modified for any body, any age, any ability.

03

Integrated Care

Cowboy Yoga is part of Cowboy Healthcare. Your yoga practice can connect directly with your broader care plan and clinical team.
