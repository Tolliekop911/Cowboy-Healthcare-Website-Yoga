What It Is

Movement as
transformation.

Buti Yoga is not a typical yoga class. It blends traditional yoga postures with tribal dance rhythms, plyometric cardio bursts, and deep core spiraling movements — all set to high-energy music that drives the intensity.

The signature element of Buti is the spiral structure technique: a method of engaging the deep core muscles through circular, spiraling movements that few other fitness modalities target. This activates the transverse abdominis, obliques, and pelvic floor in ways that conventional crunches and planks cannot.

A typical class moves through standing flows, floor sequences, dance cardio intervals, and strength-based yoga holds. You'll sweat, you'll move intuitively, and you'll finish with both a physical and emotional release that is genuinely unlike any other workout.

No dance experience is necessary. The movements are primal and instinctive — the kind your body already knows how to make.

What You'll Get

Sweat. Spiral.
Transform.

Deep Core Activation

The spiral structure technique targets the deep stabilising muscles of the core — transverse abdominis, obliques, and pelvic floor — that conventional ab workouts rarely reach.

Cardio Endurance

Plyometric bursts and dance sequences keep your heart rate elevated for sustained periods, improving cardiovascular fitness and burning high levels of calories per session.

Full-Body Strength

Yoga-based holds, bodyweight squats, and dance patterns challenge every major muscle group. You build functional strength without any equipment.

Body Confidence

Buti encourages intuitive, expressive movement that breaks the self-consciousness most people feel in conventional fitness classes. Students consistently report feeling more at home in their bodies.

Emotional Release

The combination of physical intensity, music, and movement creates a space for emotional processing. Many students describe a sense of release and clarity that outlasts the physical benefits.

Community Energy

Buti classes have a distinctive collective energy. The shared movement and music create a sense of connection that motivates you through the hardest moments of each session.
