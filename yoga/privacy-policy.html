Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

Cowboy Healthcare, LLC ("Cowboy Healthcare," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Cowboy Healthcare Physical Therapy and Cowboy Yoga websites and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our websites or use our services.

By using our websites or services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you contact us or book an appointment.
  • Appointment and booking data — dates, times, service preferences, and other scheduling details submitted through our booking system.
  • Health information — only when voluntarily provided in the context of scheduling physical therapy or yoga sessions. This information is handled in accordance with applicable healthcare privacy laws.
  • Usage data — browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referring URL, collected automatically via standard web server logs and cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Schedule, confirm, and manage appointments and class bookings
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send appointment reminders and service-related communications
  • Improve the performance and content of our websites
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Health Information (HIPAA)

As a physical therapy provider, Cowboy Healthcare is subject to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Health information you share in the context of receiving physical therapy services is protected under our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, which is available upon request and provided at your first appointment. Our electronic health records (EHR) system processes data according to its own privacy and security standards.

Cookies

Our websites use cookies to remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some features of the site may not function correctly without them. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our websites use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • EHR / Booking system — appointment scheduling and patient records are processed through our electronic health record provider.
  • Google Fonts — fonts loaded from Google's CDN; Google may collect usage data per their privacy policy.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you by contacting us at info@cowboyhealthcare.com. Requests related to health records are processed under HIPAA guidelines.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
