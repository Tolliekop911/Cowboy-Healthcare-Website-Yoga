Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

Cowboy Healthcare, LLC ("Cowboy Healthcare," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Cowboy Yoga studio and its associated website. We are committed to protecting the privacy of every person who interacts with our studio — whether as a class member, website visitor, Companion Pass holder, job applicant, or someone simply inquiring about our services.

This Privacy Policy describes in detail what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how it is used and stored, with whom it may be shared, and what rights you have over your information. Please read it carefully. By using our website or services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

This policy applies to cowboyhealthcare.com/yoga and all associated pages. It does not apply to third-party websites linked from our site.

Information We Collect

We collect information directly from you, automatically as you browse, and through the third-party tools we use to operate the studio.

Information you provide directly:

  • Contact details — full name, email address, phone number, and address when you book a class, contact us, or submit a form.
  • Class booking and membership data — class preferences, pass type (drop-in, class pack, Companion Pass), booking dates, attendance history, and any notes submitted through our scheduling system.
  • Companion Pass information — if you hold a Companion Pass, we collect the name and contact details of the companion you designate, solely for the purpose of managing shared class credits and access.
  • Payment information — payment transactions are processed by our third-party payment processor. We do not store full card numbers or CVV codes on our systems.
  • Health disclosures — if you voluntarily share information about an injury, medical condition, or physical limitation in order to receive appropriate modifications during class, we treat this with the same care as health information.
  • Employment applications — resume, CV, certifications, work history, and contact information submitted through our careers page or via email.
  • Communications — the content of any email, message, or inquiry you send to us.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage and device data — IP address, browser type and version, operating system, pages visited, time on page, and referring URL, collected via standard web server logs.
  • Cookies — small data files stored in your browser to support site functionality. See the Cookies section below.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process class bookings, confirm reservations, and manage class credits and pass balances
  • To administer Companion Pass accounts, including tracking shared class credits
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders, schedule updates, and cancellation notices
  • To process payments and issue refunds where applicable
  • To respond to inquiries, feedback, and support requests
  • To evaluate employment applications and contact candidates
  • To improve our class offerings, website experience, and studio operations
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse of our services

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Companion Pass — Privacy Note

The Companion Pass is a shared membership allowing two individuals to draw from a single pool of 20 monthly classes. When you designate a companion, you confirm that you have obtained their consent to share their name and contact information with us for the sole purpose of managing the shared pass. Companion information is used only in connection with your account and is not shared further or used for marketing without explicit consent.

Health Disclosures

Cowboy Yoga operates as part of Cowboy Healthcare's integrated wellness model. If you voluntarily disclose health or injury information to an instructor for the purpose of receiving appropriate modifications, that information is treated confidentially and used solely to provide safe, appropriate instruction. It is not stored in a formal medical record unless you also receive physical therapy services through Cowboy Healthcare, in which case HIPAA applies. See the Cowboy Healthcare Physical Therapy Privacy Policy for full HIPAA disclosures.

How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — scheduling and booking platforms, payment processors, email communication tools, and website hosting providers who assist in operating our studio. All are required to protect your data.
  • Cowboy Healthcare Physical Therapy — if you access both yoga and PT services, relevant information may be shared internally to coordinate your care, with your knowledge.
  • Legal compliance — when required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or business sale, your information may transfer to a successor entity subject to equivalent protections.

We never sell data to brokers, ad networks, or any third party for their own commercial use.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to support functionality and improve your experience. We use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to work, including session state and form submissions. Cannot be disabled without breaking site functionality.
  • Functional cookies — remember your display preferences across visits.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how pages are used so we can improve them. We use privacy-respecting, non-cross-site analytics only.

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or social media tracking scripts. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though essential cookies are required for booking functionality.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law:

  • Booking and membership records — retained for three (3) years after your last class or pass expiry for accounting, dispute resolution, and service continuity.
  • Payment records — retained for seven (7) years to satisfy tax and accounting obligations.
  • General inquiries and communications — retained for up to two (2) years.
  • Job applications — retained for one (1) year after the role is filled, then securely deleted unless you consent to longer retention.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information through administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS on all website pages
  • Access controls limiting staff access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Use of reputable, security-vetted third-party platforms for booking and payment
  • Regular review of our data handling practices

No system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee that unauthorised third parties will never defeat our security measures. If a security incident affects your data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access — request a copy of what we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to restrict processing — ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format where feasible.
  • Right to opt out — unsubscribe from non-essential communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email, or by contacting us directly.

We will respond to all rights requests within 30 days. To make a request, email info@cowboyhealthcare.com with the subject line "Privacy Request."

Children's Privacy

Our yoga classes and website are intended for adults aged 18 and over, or minors aged 13–17 with parental or guardian consent and supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with information without consent, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party sites, including our class booking platform and the Cowboy Healthcare PT website. Each has its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any website we do not control.

Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page from time to time. Continued use of our services after a change is posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
