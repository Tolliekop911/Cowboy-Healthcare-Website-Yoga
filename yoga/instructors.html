Morgan Gallagher
Lead Yoga Instructor

Morgan Gallagher is the lead yoga instructor at Cowboy Yoga, bringing a dynamic and deeply intentional approach to every class she teaches. With a background rooted in movement, anatomy, and therapeutic practice, Morgan designs each session to meet students exactly where they are — whether they are stepping onto a mat for the first time or working through recovery from injury or surgery.

Her classes blend strength, breath work, and mindful flow to create an experience that is both challenging and restorative. Morgan believes yoga is a tool for healing, not just exercise, and she works closely with Cowboy Healthcare's physical therapy team to ensure every class supports the clinical goals of the patients who attend.

Outside of the studio, Morgan is passionate about functional movement, continuing education, and building a community where every body feels welcome and capable.
