Meet your instructors

Our instructors bring clinical expertise and genuine passion for movement to every class.

Morgan Gallagher
Lead Yoga Instructor

Morgan Gallagher is the lead yoga instructor at Cowboy Yoga, bringing a dynamic, high-energy, and deeply intentional approach to every class she teaches.

She designs each session to meet students exactly where they are — whether they're stepping onto the mat for the first time or looking to level up their strength and endurance.

Her classes blend sculpt and buti yoga styles with strength-building flows, breath work, and mindful movement to create an experience that is both powerfully challenging and deeply empowering. Expect sculpted cores, fiery energy, dance-like rhythms, and that satisfying burn that leaves you feeling strong, confident, and alive.

Morgan believes yoga is a tool for transformation, resilience, and radiant energy — not just exercise. She is passionate about helping every body discover its power through movement.

Outside the studio, Morgan is a proud mother, daughter, wife, and loved friend. She loves traveling, dancing, fashion, coaching ice hockey, skiing, fly fishing, and rock climbing. With over 20 years of experience as a hair creator specializing in coloring, balayage, and curl management, she has a passion for all natural hair and body products. Passionate about functional movement and continuing education, she is dedicated to building a welcoming community where every student feels capable, inspired, and ready to show up fully for themselves.
