What It Is

Yoga built on
clinical science.

Therapeutic Yoga at Cowboy Healthcare is different from any other yoga class you've taken — because it's designed by and supervised by licensed Doctors of Physical Therapy. Every sequence is built around restoring movement quality, not just achieving poses.

Classes are structured around functional movement patterns: hinging, squatting, rotating, reaching. Each session improves mobility at joints that are commonly restricted after injury, surgery, or long periods of inactivity, while simultaneously building the stability and strength needed to protect those joints.

This class is ideal for anyone managing a chronic condition, recovering from injury or surgery, dealing with persistent pain, or simply returning to movement after a long time away. Modifications are built into every posture so that participants at any stage of recovery can participate safely and effectively.

Because our instructors are also licensed PTs, they can identify movement compensations in real time and provide individualised cues that a standard yoga teacher would not be trained to give.

What You'll Get

Movement that
heals.

Pain Management

Targeted mobility and strengthening sequences reduce the mechanical stress on joints and tissues that drives chronic pain — addressing root causes, not masking symptoms.

Post-Injury Recovery

Whether you're post-op or post-acute, Therapeutic Yoga bridges the gap between formal PT and returning to full activity — with clinical guidance every step of the way.

Joint Mobility

Systematic mobility work at the hips, thoracic spine, shoulders, and ankles restores range of motion that pain, inactivity, or surgery have taken away.

Stability & Strength

Mobility without stability is injury waiting to happen. Each class builds the neuromuscular control needed to move well and protect newly regained range of motion.

Nervous System Regulation

Breathwork and mindful movement downregulate the central sensitisation that amplifies chronic pain — a mechanism that conventional PT often doesn't address directly.

DPT Supervision

Our instructors hold Doctorate of Physical Therapy degrees. They can modify in real time based on your individual presentation — a clinical advantage no standard yoga studio offers.
