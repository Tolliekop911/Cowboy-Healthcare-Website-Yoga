Yoga Instructor

Part-Time or Full-Time  ·  Ravenna, OH  ·  On-Site

About the Role

Cowboy Yoga is looking for a knowledgeable, energetic yoga instructor who can lead engaging classes for students of all levels. You will work closely with our physical therapy team to offer movement-based programming that bridges wellness and rehabilitation.

Whether you specialize in Vinyasa, Hatha, Restorative, or therapeutic yoga — we want to hear from you. Class formats and scheduling are flexible and built around your availability.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and lead group yoga classes (mixed levels and specialty formats)
  • Create a safe, welcoming environment for students of all backgrounds and abilities
  • Offer modifications and hands-on assists as appropriate
  • Collaborate with our PT team on injury-aware programming
  • Maintain accurate attendance records and communicate scheduling needs
  • Participate in occasional community events and workshops

Requirements

  • 200-hour RYT certification minimum (500-hour preferred)
  • 1+ year of teaching experience in a studio or clinical setting
  • CPR/First Aid certified (or willing to obtain)
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills
  • Punctual, professional, and reliable

Nice to Have

  • Background in therapeutic, prenatal, or adaptive yoga
  • Experience working alongside physical therapists or healthcare providers
  • Speciality certifications (Yin, Aerial, Chair Yoga, etc.)

Compensation

Competitive per-class rate, commensurate with experience and certification level. Full-time candidates may be eligible for additional benefits. Flexible scheduling with both morning and evening class slots available.

Ready to apply?

Send your CV, a brief cover letter, and proof of certification to info@cowboyhealthcare.com with the subject line "Yoga Instructor Application — Cowboy Yoga". We review applications on a rolling basis and will be in touch within 5–7 business days.
